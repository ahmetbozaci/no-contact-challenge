Safety & Crisis Disclaimer

Important: If you are in immediate danger, feel unsafe, or may harm yourself or someone else, contact local emergency services or a trusted person now. This website cannot provide emergency help.

Not professional care

No Contact Challenge is a self-support and habit-tracking tool. It is not a replacement for therapy, crisis counseling, medical care, legal help, or domestic violence support.

When to seek help

  • You feel at risk of harming yourself or someone else.
  • You are being threatened, stalked, controlled, or abused.
  • You feel unable to stay safe alone.
  • You need legal or protective support.

No-shame reset policy

Breaking no-contact does not mean you are a failure. The app should help users restart gently, notice triggers, and make a safer next plan.

Privacy reminder

Avoid writing details that could endanger you if seen by someone else. Use privacy settings, strong passwords, and a safe device.
