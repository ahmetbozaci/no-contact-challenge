Terms of Use

These terms are a starter template for the No Contact Challenge prototype. They should be reviewed before any public release.

Purpose of the app

The app provides habit tracking, reflection, and community encouragement for people practicing no-contact. It is not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, or emergency support.

User responsibilities

  • Use the app respectfully and do not harass, impersonate, or abuse other users.
  • Do not post identifying, threatening, illegal, hateful, or harmful content.
  • Protect your account and use a strong password.
  • Do not try to access another person’s private data.

Community features

Community visibility is controlled by privacy settings. Admins may hide, rename, suspend, or delete accounts that appear abusive or unsafe.

Availability

This prototype may change, lose local data, or be unavailable. A production version should include backups, monitoring, and stronger infrastructure.

Account deletion

Users may delete their account from settings. Production deployments should clearly describe whether deleted data may remain in backups for a limited period.

Changes

Terms may be updated as the product changes. Public users should be notified of major changes.
