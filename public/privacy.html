Privacy Policy

This app is designed for private healing support. It may store sensitive personal notes, reflections, urges, relapse logs, message drafts, reminders, profile settings, and account details.

Prototype notice: this local project stores data in a server-side JSON file. For a real public launch, use a production database, HTTPS, backups, and a reviewed privacy policy.

Information collected

  • Account information such as email, password hash, display name, and profile preferences.
  • Challenge data such as check-ins, streaks, milestones, reflections, urge logs, message graveyard entries, reasons started, and reset/relapse notes.
  • Community data such as public display name, visible streak/status if enabled, encouragement counts, and reports submitted to admins.
  • Technical data such as session cookies and basic security/rate-limit records.

How information is used

  • To provide check-ins, progress tracking, privacy controls, reminders, resources, and account features.
  • To prevent abuse, moderate reports, and protect community safety.
  • To support account recovery and email verification when a real email provider is connected.

Privacy controls

You can choose whether to appear in the community dashboard and whether to show streaks, last check-in, mood, milestones, and encouragement options. Private notes and message graveyard content are not shown publicly.

Data export and deletion

The account settings area includes export and delete-account controls. Deletion should remove the account and associated private data from the app storage. Production systems should also define backup-retention behavior.

Cookies

The app uses an HTTP-only session cookie to keep you signed in. Browser notification settings may be stored locally on your device.
